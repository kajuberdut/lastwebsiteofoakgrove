Welcome to Oak Grove

Sixteen-year-old Morgan never asked to be the last wizard of Oak Grove. After her father’s untimely death, she inherits the responsibility of maintaining the magical barrier that keeps her small town safe from the Fae realm—a barrier that has protected Oak Grove for over a century.

Caught between the pressures of school, friendships, and her grief, Morgan struggles to live up to her father’s legacy. But as attacks from the Thinning intensify, she discovers a deeper plot led by Caelia, the changeling daughter of Oberon, King of the Fae. With the balance between worlds at stake, Morgan must uncover her own strength, or risk losing everything.

The Crow “Sadness”

Morgan’s magical construct to contain her grief, the Crow embodies her suppressed emotions. Yet, it holds a key to her most potent power—if she dares to confront it.

The Thinning

A fragile boundary between realms, the Thinning pulses with dangerous magic. It’s a place where the mundane and the mystical collide, testing the courage of anyone who dares to enter.

Oberon, King of the Fae

The ancient and enigmatic ruler of the Fae, Oberon’s wisdom and authority shape the delicate balance between Oak Grove and the Thinning.

The Dark One

A shadowy force of malevolence, the Dark One seeks to engulf Oak Grove in darkness, feeding on fear and doubt to weaken the magical barrier.
